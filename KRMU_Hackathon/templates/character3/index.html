Introduction

Legends speak of the night she ascended—the sky bled crimson as her power swallowed the heavens. With a single word, she shattered the celestial chains that bound her kind, unleashing an age of terror upon the mortal realm. But behind her crimson gaze lies a deeper purpose—vengeance against those who betrayed her and the promise of a new world, reborn in darkness.

In the forgotten abyss where the stars dare not shine, Kaelith, the Demon Empress, rose from the ashes of a shattered empire. Once a forsaken heir to a ruined kingdom, she forged a pact with the Void, embracing the shadows that whispered her name. Now, cloaked in an aura of darkness, she commands an army of wraiths and nightmares, her mere presence twisting reality itself.

Will you stand against the Empress… or kneel before the abyss.
