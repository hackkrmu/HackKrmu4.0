Artificial Intelligence Supported Sign Language Translator System

We enable hearing impaired and deaf individuals who have difficulty in understanding what they read or are illiterate to access information, services, and video content with the AI-powered sign language, which is their mother tongue.

About us

We provide practical, entrepreneurial, and innovative services to improve the quality of life for individuals with disabilities in essential areas like education, banking, insurance, and communication.

Our Objective

In today's technology world, we advocate that everyone has the right to access information and services and that everyone should live under equal conditions. That's why we are developing artificial intelligence-supported digital accessibility solutions to eliminate the problems experienced by hearing impaired and deaf individuals who have difficulty in understanding what they read, and in accessing services and information in digital environments!
